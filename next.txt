 1. Bing Webmaster Tools + IndexNow

  Bing powers DuckDuckGo, Yahoo, ChatGPT browsing, and Copilot. You're missing 5-10% of traffic
  without it.

  - Sign up at https://www.bing.com/webmasters
  - Import directly from GSC — they have a one-click import; no need to re-add 53 URLs
  - Submit your sitemap (https://thehisaab.com/sitemap.xml)
  - Enable IndexNow — one ping notifies Bing, Yandex, Naver, Seznam instantly when you publish or
  update a page. Cuts weeks off discovery.

  2. AI bot crawl access — robots.txt + llms.txt

  Right now your robots.txt may be blocking or silent on the AI crawlers. Explicitly allow them. Also
  create llms.txt (emerging standard) — it's the AI-equivalent of a sitemap, and
  ChatGPT/Perplexity/Claude pay attention to it.

  This is small code work — I can ship it.

  3. AlternativeTo.net listing

  ChatGPT cited it. AlternativeTo ranks high for every "X alternative" search. Submit The Hisaab as a
  listed alternative under Splitwise, Splitkaro, Tricount, Settle Up. It's free, takes 15 minutes, and
   gives you a high-DA backlink.

  4. Schema.org Article + HowTo markup on blog posts

  Your blog posts currently only have FAQPage schema (the layout-level FAQs). Adding Article schema
  bumps Google search appearance to "rich result" status — author, date, image — and helps LLM
  extraction. HowTo schema on the how-to-split-rent and migration posts gets you Google's step-by-step
   rich result.
